# Product Overview

SPIDER is a contractor marketplace platform that connects customers with verified contractors for construction, renovation, interior design, repairs, and smart home installations.

## Key Features

- **Random Match System**: Unique algorithm that matches customers with 1-3 qualified contractors based on service type, location, budget, and availability
- **Milestone-Based Payments**: Project tracking through defined milestones with integrated payment processing
- **Multi-User Dashboards**: Specialized interfaces for customers, contractors, coordinators, sales reps, and admins
- **Smart Home Integration**: Product catalog for solar panels, EV chargers, and smart devices with installation services

## Target Markets

- Primary: Thai market with Thai/English language support
- Services: Construction, renovation, interior design, repairs, smart home installations
- Users: Homeowners, contractors, back-office staff

## Business Model

- Lead generation and contractor matching
- Commission-based revenue from completed projects
- Premium contractor listings and features
- Smart home product sales with installation services

## Core Value Propositions

- **For Customers**: Vetted contractors, transparent pricing, milestone-based protection
- **For Contractors**: Quality leads, business management tools, fair matching system
- **For Platform**: Scalable marketplace with automated matching and comprehensive oversight
